Our Services

We specialise in nothing but our core skill as Building Surveyors which enables a practical approach to bring together the disciplines of legal requirements, design and engineering. Our traditional approach and long period of training and expertise provide the best overview of property problems to enhance value and provide advice on present condition and future issues.

We have particular expertise in providing surveys for a variety of reasons but particularly relating to high quality residential premises. Our surveyors have been involved in property transactions on some of the highest value residential buildings in the world. Similarly we are retained by many commercial organisations to assist them in their various property transactions and needs.

Our main expertise is in:

  • Building surveys of high value residential properties
  • Party Wall Awards
  • Dilapidations
  • Refurbishment, design and contract management.

Our skills enable us to offer specialist advice in particular on:

  • Housing and Residential properties
  • Retail Shops and Warehouses
  • Commercial and Industrial Premises
  • Licensed and Entertainment premises
  • Restaurant and A3 premises

Our Services are divided into Consultancies comprising:

  1. Building Surveys
  2. Development & Legislation
  3. Design & Construction
  4. Professional Consultancy
